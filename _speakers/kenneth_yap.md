Hoong Hao, who also goes by Kenneth, is a Doctoral Scholar at the EPSRC Centre for Doctoral Training in Future Infrastructure and Built Environment (FIBE2 CDT). His research centres on integrating Digital Twins (DT) and Multi-Agent Systems (MAS) to improve inter- and intra-organizational decision-making. Under the supervision of Dr. Karel Kruger, Kenneth is based at the Distributed Information & Automation Laboratory (DIAL) within the Institute for Manufacturing (IfM).

Kenneth holds a Master of Engineering degree from Imperial College London and ETH Zürich, a Master of Research from the University of Cambridge, and a Master of Financial Engineering from WorldQuant University. He has been awarded the Associateship of the City and Guilds Institute and is currently working towards an Associate Fellowship of the Higher Education Academy.

More information about Kenneth’s research and accomplishments can be found on his GitHub page: https://kenneth-yap.vercel.app/.
